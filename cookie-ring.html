Ring Collector Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Just like Sonic collects rings, our website collects cookies! These are small text files stored on your device that help us provide you with a better browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Like different power-ups in the game, we use different types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Like the invincibility star, these are necessary for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Like speed boots, these help us understand how you use our website
  • Functionality Cookies: Like special abilities, these remember your preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Like score counters, these help us measure website performance

Cookie Control

Just as you control Mario and Sonic in the game, you can control cookies in your browser:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Block non-essential cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set up cookie preferences

Third-Party Cookies

Like team-up moves, some features on our website use third-party cookies. These are placed by external services we use to enhance your experience.

Cookie Duration

Like power-ups, cookies have different durations:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary, like temporary invincibility
  • Persistent Cookies: Long-lasting, like collected extra lives

Updates to This Policy

Like game updates, we may update this cookie policy from time to time. Please check back regularly to stay informed about how we use cookies.

Questions?
